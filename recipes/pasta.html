Buttered Pasta

This is a recipe for buttered pasta. It is the pinnacle dish of the italian world.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Pasta
  • Salt
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Fill a large pot with water. It really doesn't matter how much as long as you can cover all the pasta.
  2. Bring the water to a boil and add a 1.5 teaspoons of salt. It needs to be salty like the sea.
  3. Add a whole box of pasta to you pot and stir it for a few seconds.
  4. Boil for about 8 minutes (give or take). All pasta is different, so the time will vary.
  5. Test the pasta with a fork and see if it's a good consistency.
  6. Strain and add butter.
